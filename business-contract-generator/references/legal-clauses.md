## Payment and Financial Terms

### Payment Schedule Clause

```
2.1 Payment Terms
(a) The Client shall pay the Fees according to the payment schedule below:
    - Upon execution of this Agreement: [X]%
    - Upon completion of [milestone]: [X]%
    - Upon final delivery and acceptance: [X]%

(b) All payments shall be made within [X] business days of invoice receipt
to the Service Provider's designated bank account.

(c) Invoices must include:
    - Invoice number and date
    - Description of services rendered
    - Amount due
    - Payment terms
    - Service Provider's bank details
```

### Late Payment Clause

```
2.2 Late Payment
(a) If any payment is not received by the due date, interest shall accrue
at the rate of [X]% per annum or the maximum rate permitted by law,
whichever is lower.

(b) If payment is overdue by more than [X] days, the Service Provider may,
at its discretion and without limiting other remedies:
    (i) Suspend performance of services
    (ii) Terminate this Agreement with [X] days written notice
```

### Price Adjustment Clause

```
2.3 Price Adjustment
(a) The Fees are fixed for the initial [X] months of the Agreement term.

(b) After [X] months, Fees may be adjusted annually on [date] to reflect
changes in:
    - Operating costs
    - Labor costs
    - Materials costs
    - Market conditions

(c) The Service Provider shall provide written notice of any proposed
adjustment at least [X] days in advance.
```

### Currency and Exchange Rate Clause

```
2.4 Currency and Exchange Rate
(a) All payments shall be made in Chinese Renminbi (RMB).

(b) If payment is made in another currency, the exchange rate shall be
determined by [specify source] on the payment date.

(c) The Party making payment shall bear all currency conversion costs
and bank charges.
```

## Liability and Indemnification

### Limitation of Liability Clause

```
6.1 Limitation of Liability
(a) EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS, INFRINGEMENT OF
THIRD-PARTY INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS, OR GROSS NEGLIGENCE OR WILLFUL
MISCONDUCT, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR:
    (i) Any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages
    (ii) Loss of profits, revenue, data, or goodwill
    (iii) Cost of substitute services

(b) Each party's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed
the total Fees paid (or payable) in the [X] months preceding the claim.

(c) This limitation applies to all claims, whether based on warranty,
contract, tort, or any other legal theory.
```

### Indemnification Clause - Service Provider

```
6.2 Indemnification by Service Provider
(a) The Service Provider shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless
the Client from any third-party claims, damages, and costs (including
reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:
    (i) Infringement of any patent, copyright, trademark, or other
        intellectual property right
    (ii) Violation of applicable laws or regulations
    (iii) The Service Provider's gross negligence or willful misconduct

(b) The Service Provider's indemnification obligation is conditioned upon
the Client:
    (i) Providing prompt written notice of the claim
    (ii) Granting the Service Provider sole control of defense and settlement
    (iii) Providing reasonable cooperation in the defense
```

### Indemnification Clause - Client

```
6.3 Indemnification by Client
(a) The Client shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Service
Provider from any third-party claims, damages, and costs (including
reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:
    (i) Client-provided materials or content that infringe third-party
        intellectual property rights
    (ii) The Client's breach of this Agreement
    (iii) The Client's violation of applicable laws
```

## Intellectual Property Rights

### IP Ownership - Work Product Clause

```
4.1 Ownership of Work Product
(a) All work product, deliverables, and materials created by the Service
Provider specifically for the Client under this Agreement ("Work Product")
shall be the exclusive property of the Client upon full payment of all Fees.

(b) The Client shall have all rights to use, modify, reproduce, and
distribute the Work Product.

(c) The Service Provider retains ownership of:
    - Pre-existing tools, methodologies, and know-how
    - General knowledge and experience gained
    - Derivative works not specifically created for Client
```

### Pre-Existing IP Clause

```
4.2 Pre-Existing Intellectual Property
(a) Each party retains all right, title, and interest in its
pre-existing intellectual property, materials, and know-how.

(b) The Service Provider hereby grants the Client a non-exclusive,
royalty-free, perpetual license to use any pre-existing intellectual
property incorporated into the Work Product.

(c) The license does not include rights to:
    - Create derivative works from the pre-existing IP
    - Remove any proprietary notices
    - Use the pre-existing IP for other purposes
```

### Confidential Information in IP Clause

```
4.3 Confidential Information
(a) The Service Provider may use Client's confidential information solely
for the purpose of performing services under this Agreement.

(b) Upon completion or termination, the Service Provider shall:
    (i) Return or destroy all Client confidential information
    (ii) Certify compliance in writing
    (iii) Delete all copies from computer systems
```

## Confidentiality

### Confidentiality Obligation Clause

```
5.1 Confidential Information
(a) "Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed
by one party to the other, including but not limited to:
    - Business plans, strategies, and financial information
    - Technical data, source code, and specifications
    - Customer lists and pricing information
    - Any information marked as "Confidential"

(b) Confidential Information does NOT include information that:
    (i) Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement
    (ii) Was rightfully known prior to disclosure
    (iii) Is rightfully received from a third party without confidentiality
         obligation
    (iv) Is independently developed without use of the Confidential Information
```

### Permitted Disclosure Clause

```
5.2 Permitted Disclosure
(a) The receiving party may disclose Confidential Information:
    (i) To employees, contractors, and agents with a need to know
    (ii) As required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
    (iii) To legal counsel for legal advice

(b) When disclosure is required by law, the receiving party shall:
    (i) Provide prompt notice to the disclosing party
    (ii) Reasonably cooperate in obtaining a protective order
    (iii) Disclose only the minimum information required
```

### Duration of Confidentiality Clause

```
5.3 Duration
(a) Confidentiality obligations survive for [X] years after termination
of this Agreement.

(b) Trade secrets shall remain confidential for so long as they qualify
as trade secrets under applicable law.

(c) Either party may terminate confidentiality obligations for information
that becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement.
```

## Termination Provisions

### Termination for Cause Clause

```
7.1 Termination for Cause
(a) Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written
notice if the other party:
    (i) Materially breaches any obligation and fails to cure within
        [X] days of written notice
    (ii) Becomes insolvent or bankrupt
    (iii) Abandons performance for more than [X] days without valid reason

(b) The terminating party shall provide written notice specifying:
    - The alleged breach
    - The required corrective action
    - The deadline for cure
```

### Termination for Convenience Clause

```
7.2 Termination for Convenience
(a) Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon
[X] days written notice.

(b) Upon such termination:
    (i) The Client shall pay for all work completed and costs incurred
    (ii) The Service Provider shall deliver all work product and
         Confidential Information
    (iii) Survival provisions remain in effect
```

### Termination Effects Clause

```
7.3 Effects of Termination
(a) Upon termination, regardless of cause:
    (i) The Client shall pay all undisputed invoices
    (ii) The Service Provider shall cease work and return materials
    (iii) Confidentiality obligations survive termination
    (iv) Limitation of liability provisions survive termination

(b) Termination does not relieve either party of obligations accrued
before the termination date.

(c) Upon termination, the Client shall have the right to use work product
in progress, subject to payment of reasonable costs.
```

## Dispute Resolution

### Governing Law Clause

```
8.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of the People's Republic of China, without regard to its
conflict of law provisions.
```

### Arbitration Clause

```
8.2 Arbitration
(a) Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be
finally resolved by binding arbitration administered by the
[China International Economic and Trade Arbitration Commission /
Beijing Arbitration Commission / Shanghai Arbitration Commission].

(b) The arbitration shall be:
    (i) Conducted in [Chinese / English]
    (ii) Conducted by [one / three] arbitrator(s)
    (iii) Held in [Beijing / Shanghai / other city]
    (iv) Conducted according to the Commission's Rules of Procedure

(c) The arbitration award shall be final and binding and enforceable
in any court of competent jurisdiction.

(d) Each party shall bear its own costs and share the arbitration fees equally.
```

### Litigation Clause

```
8.3 Litigation
Any dispute not resolved through negotiation shall be brought in the
competent courts of [specify jurisdiction] in the People's Republic of China.
```

### Negotiation and Mediation Clause

```
8.4 Negotiation and Mediation
(a) Before initiating formal dispute resolution, the parties shall attempt
to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation.

(b) If negotiation fails within [X] days, either party may initiate mediation
with a mutually agreed mediator.

(c) The mediation process shall be:
    (i) Confidential
    (ii) Non-binding
    (iii) Completed within [X] days unless extended by mutual consent

(d) If mediation is unsuccessful, either party may proceed to arbitration
or litigation as specified in this Agreement.
```

## Force Majeure

### Force Majeure Clause

```
9.1 Force Majeure
(a) "Force Majeure Event" means any event beyond the reasonable control
of a party, including but not limited to:
    - Acts of God (earthquake, flood, hurricane, epidemic, etc.)
    - War, terrorism, or government action
    - Strikes or labor disputes
    - Utility failures
    - Pandemic or public health emergency
    - Internet or telecommunications failures

(b) If a party is prevented from performing its obligations due to a
Force Majeure Event:
    (i) The party shall provide written notice within [X] days
    (ii) The party shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact
    (iii) Performance obligations shall be suspended during the Force Majeure Event

(c) If a Force Majeure Event prevents performance for more than [X] days,
either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice without liability.

(d) This clause does not excuse payment obligations for work already completed.
```

## General Provisions

### Entire Agreement Clause

```
10.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement, including all exhibits and attachments, constitutes the
entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter and
supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements, whether
written or oral.
```

### Amendment and Modification Clause

```
10.2 Amendments
(a) This Agreement may be amended, modified, or supplemented only by
written document signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

(b) No waiver of any provision or right is effective unless in writing
and signed by the waiving party.

(c) Waiver of any breach does not constitute waiver of any other breach.
```

### Severability Clause

```
10.3 Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, illegal, or
unenforceable by a court of competent jurisdiction, that provision shall
be modified to the minimum extent necessary to make it valid and enforceable,
or if not possible, severed. The remaining provisions shall remain in full
force and effect.
```

### Counterparts Clause

```
10.4 Counterparts
This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be
deemed an original and all of which together shall constitute one instrument.
Facsimile or PDF signatures shall be as binding as original signatures.
```

### Assignment Clause

```
10.5 Assignment
(a) Neither party may assign its rights or obligations under this Agreement
without the prior written consent of the other party, except as permitted
by law.

(b) Any attempted assignment in violation of this provision is void.

(c) [Optional: For acquisitions or mergers, either party may assign without
consent to an acquirer or successor.]
```

### Notices Clause

```
10.6 Notices
All notices required under this Agreement shall be:
    (a) In writing
    (b) Delivered by:
        (i) Hand delivery
        (ii) Certified mail
        (iii) Email with read receipt
    (c) Sent to the addresses specified below:

    Service Provider: [Address]
    Client: [Address]

Notice shall be effective upon receipt.
```
