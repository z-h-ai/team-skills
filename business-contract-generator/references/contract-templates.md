## Service Contract Template

### Structure for Service Contracts

```
1. Preamble
   - Parties and representative details
   - Effective date

2. Service Scope
   - Description of services
   - Scope of work and deliverables
   - Service performance standards

3. Service Term
   - Start date and duration
   - Renewal conditions
   - Termination notice period

4. Payment Terms
   - Fee structure and pricing
   - Payment schedule and method
   - Invoicing procedures
   - Late payment penalties

5. Intellectual Property
   - Ownership of work product
   - Pre-existing IP rights
   - Confidential information handling

6. Liability and Indemnification
   - Limitation of liability
   - Indemnification clauses
   - Insurance requirements

7. Confidentiality
   - Definition of confidential information
   - Permitted disclosure
   - Duration of confidentiality

8. Termination
   - Termination conditions
   - Termination procedures
   - Effects of termination

9. Dispute Resolution
   - Governing law
   - Jurisdiction and venue
   - Mediation/arbitration procedures

10. General Provisions
    - Amendments and modifications
    - Entire agreement clause
    - Severability
    - Force majeure
```

## Purchase Agreement Template

### Structure for Purchase Agreements

```
1. Preamble
   - Buyer and seller information
   - Date and effective period

2. Products/Services Description
   - Detailed specifications
   - Models and specifications
   - Quantity and unit pricing

3. Total Price and Payment Terms
   - Unit price and total price
   - Payment schedule
   - Payment method
   - Currency and exchange rate (if applicable)

4. Delivery Terms
   - Delivery location
   - Delivery date/period
   - Risk of loss
   - Transportation and insurance

5. Quality Assurance
   - Inspection procedures
   - Acceptance criteria
   - Warranty period
   - Non-conforming goods handling

6. Title and Risk Transfer
   - When title transfers
   - When risk of loss transfers
   - Insurance responsibility

7. Warranty
   - Express warranties
   - Warranty period
   - Warranty disclaimer and limitations
   - Remedy for warranty breach

8. Returns and Refunds
   - Return procedures
   - Return period
   - Restocking fees (if applicable)
   - Refund conditions

9. Intellectual Property
   - Ownership of patents and designs
   - License grants
   - Third-party IP indemnification

10. Dispute Resolution
    - Governing law
    - Dispute resolution mechanism
    - Jurisdiction and arbitration
```

## Collaboration Agreement Template

### Structure for Collaboration Agreements

```
1. Preamble
   - Names and details of all parties
   - Purpose and objectives
   - Effective date

2. Scope of Collaboration
   - Joint project description
   - Goals and milestones
   - Territory and market scope

3. Contributions
   - Financial contributions
   - Personnel and resources
   - Technology and IP contributions
   - Timeline for contributions

4. Management and Decision-Making
   - Management structure
   - Decision-making authority
   - Regular reporting and meetings
   - Authority limits and escalation

5. Revenue and Cost Sharing
   - Revenue sharing formula
   - Cost allocation
   - Profit/loss distribution
   - Financial accounting and audit

6. Intellectual Property
   - Ownership of jointly created IP
   - Use of pre-existing IP
   - Confidential information
   - IP protection and enforcement

7. Warranties and Representations
   - Authority to enter agreement
   - No conflicting obligations
   - Compliance with laws

8. Confidentiality
   - Definition of confidential information
   - Use restrictions
   - Disclosure obligations
   - Survival after termination

9. Duration and Termination
   - Collaboration period
   - Renewal conditions
   - Termination rights and procedures
   - Effects of termination
   - Wind-up procedures

10. Dispute Resolution
    - Dispute resolution procedures
    - Governing law
    - Jurisdiction and arbitration

11. General Provisions
    - Amendment procedures
    - Force majeure
    - Severability
    - Entire agreement
```

## General Contract Elements

### Standard Opening Clause

All contracts should begin with a clear preamble identifying:
- Party names and business addresses
- Effective date
- Contract purpose
- Governing jurisdiction (China)

### Standard Closing Clause

All contracts should include:
- Signature blocks with dates
- Authority confirmation
- Language (typically Chinese with English translation if needed)
- Number of copies (typically 2-3)
- Effective date and execution date
