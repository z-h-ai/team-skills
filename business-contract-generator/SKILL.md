# Business Contract Generator

## Overview

This skill helps generate professional, legally compliant business contracts under Chinese law framework. It provides pre-drafted templates, standard legal clauses, and best practices to quickly produce comprehensive contracts for various business scenarios, with full customization support for specific terms and conditions.

## Quick Start

To generate a contract:

1. **Specify the contract type** - Choose from service agreements, purchase contracts, collaboration agreements, or other contract types
2. **Provide key parties and terms** - Company names, service descriptions, payment terms, duration, liability, etc.
3. **Customize clauses** - Adjust standard clauses to fit your specific business needs
4. **Review for compliance** - Verify the contract aligns with your legal requirements

Example user request: "Generate a service contract for a software development company providing cloud infrastructure services to a client, including payment terms, liability limitations, and termination clauses."

## Generating Service Contracts

Service contracts formalize the relationship between a service provider and client. They outline deliverables, payment terms, timelines, and responsibility.

**Key elements to include:**
- Service scope and deliverables
- Performance standards and timelines
- Payment terms and pricing
- Confidentiality and IP ownership
- Liability and indemnification
- Term and termination conditions
- Dispute resolution

Prompt the user for: service type, duration, payment schedule, liability caps, and any industry-specific requirements.

See [contract-templates.md](references/contract-templates.md) for complete service contract templates covering:
- Software development services
- Consulting services
- Maintenance and support services
- Professional services

## Generating Purchase/Sales Contracts

Purchase agreements define terms for buying and selling goods or services.

**Key elements to include:**
- Product/service specifications
- Quantity and pricing
- Delivery terms and timeline
- Payment method and schedule
- Quality assurance and inspection
- Warranty and returns
- Risk of loss and insurance

See [legal-clauses.md](references/legal-clauses.md) for standard commercial clauses applicable to sales contracts.

## Generating Collaboration Agreements

Collaboration agreements establish partnerships between two or more parties for joint ventures or projects.

**Key elements to include:**
- Scope of collaboration
- Contribution from each party
- Profit/loss sharing
- Decision-making authority
- Intellectual property ownership
- Confidentiality obligations
- Exit and dispute resolution
